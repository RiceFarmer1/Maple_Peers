Volunteering in Ontario
  • Volunteering



  • This website contains a lot of information on volunteering opportunities in the Greater Toronto Area. Its goal is to help individuals find suitable volunteer positions with the aid of online resources, referral services and public awareness initiatives.

    Canadian Cancer Society

    People in communities from coast-to-coast come together throughout each year as the Canadian Cancer Society volunteers to help deliver our mission. When you volunteer with us, you become part of our collective, working together to support all Canadians with all cancers.

    Volunteer with the City of Toronto

    There are many opportunities for the public to get involved with the City of Toronto throughout the year. The City actively encourages participation by its residents in order to make our city a better place for all to live, work, learn and play.

    Toronto Wildlife

    Toronto Wildlife Centre is only able to carry out its work thanks to the time, expertise and dedication of hundreds of volunteers. Volunteers are involved in every facet of operations, including wildlife care, education, grocery shopping, and much, much more.


    Toronto Public Library

    You can help the Library by volunteering in a number of programs run by the Toronto Public Library. Take part in this rewarding experience and assist others in your community by volunteering today.

    Evergreen

    Evergreen’s volunteers are vital to make our cities flourish. They are city builders who support a host of projects and programs at Evergreen Brick Works (Toronto) and across the country. Through volunteering, you can take action and make your city better.

    Harbourfront Centre

    Harbourfront Centre’s diverse and talented group of volunteers are integral partners in the presentation of innovative contemporary arts and culture programming. They contribute resources – time, skills, expertise, and leadership – that enhance the visitors experience with professionalism, passion, and positive energy. ​
